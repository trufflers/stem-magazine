About The Centrifuge

The Centrifuge is a student-run STEM magazine that focuses on bringing clarity to complex scientific ideas. Like a camera The Centrifuge that controls what comes into focus, we help high school students frame their discoveries and share their unique perspectives on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Our Mission

To create a platform where high school students can share their research, insights, and passion for STEM with peers around the world. We aim to foster a community of young scientists and innovators who support and inspire each other.

What We Publish

We feature a wide range of content, including:

  • Technical Tutorials - How-to guides and coding projects
  • Opinion Pieces - Perspectives on scientific developments
  • Interviews - Conversations with student researchers and professionals

Why The Centrifuge?

In photography, the The Centrifuge controls depth of field—what's sharp and what fades into the background. In science, it's about choosing what to focus on, how to frame a problem, and what perspective to take. High school students bring fresh eyes to STEM, seeing details others might miss and asking questions that challenge conventions. The Centrifuge gives those perspectives a platform, bringing student discoveries into sharp focus.

Join us in celebrating student innovation and building the next generation of STEM leaders.
